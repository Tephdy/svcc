BACHELOR IN CRIMINOLOGY

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology is a (4) four-year degree program generally offered to students who are interested to pursue a career in law enforcement, correctional administration, security, and crime prevention. Students in this program will be able to learn different practices and theories of laws related to criminal behavior which leads to effective law enforcement. A graduate of this program is required to take and pass the Criminologist Licensure Examination to be considered a Criminologist. A graduate of B.S Criminology is prepared for careers in the following fields, Criminological Research, Scientific Crime Detection and Investigation, Crime Prevention, Law Enforcement, Correctional Administration, Public safety, Security Management, Criminalistics and in Academe.
