St. Vincent College of Cabuyao Roads to first-ever virtual EU-Whiz


St. Vincent College of Cabuyao is at stake at the annual EU-Whiz: Filipino Youth of the FEUture is here!

EU-Whiz is divided into three categories; Online Trivia Competition, the Digital Art Competition and the Video Competition. The said competition is open to bonafide Filipino students currently enrolled in Philippine universities for Academic Year 2021. Interested students must form a group of three members to participate. Cool gadgets and exclusive EU Whiz items await the winning teams.

SVCC's final list of delegates per category will be posted soon.
